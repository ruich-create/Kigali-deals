Our History

Founded in 2015 in Kigali, KIGALI DEALS started as a small family business helping locals find affordable housing. Today, we're Rwanda's go-to for real deals—growing from one van rental to a full suite of services with free delivery across the city.

  • 2015: Launched house rentals.
  • 2018: Added car fleet.
  • 2022: Expanded to plots and materials.

Our mission: Making prosperity accessible for all Rwandans.
